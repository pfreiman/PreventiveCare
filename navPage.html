Alternate Risk Scores


Entries do not match inclusion criteria for the ACC/AHA ASCVD Risk Score calculation.

Alternate Risk Score calculators should be used if:

  • Age is less than 40 or greater than 75 years
  • Race is other than white or african-american

Also, some risk score calculators allow the incorporation of Coronary Artery Calcium (CAC) scores and/or CRP values.
